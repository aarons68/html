Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Bowl & Fork

Process

  1. Crack the two eggs into the bowl
  2. whisk eggs in bowl with fork until yolk is broken up
  3. Put stove on medium heat with a pan with butter on top of the burner
  4. Cook eggs to liking in the pan, while scraping away sides to not stick to the pan

Glossary

Whisk
In a bowl, mix the yolk with eggwhite with either a whisk or a fork until the consistency is uniform throughout the bowl.
